Welcome To Parody

A game to mock all games

You will play as a game testing Robot trying to fix 3 games from a terrible virus

The virus has deployed bugs in each game, its your job to find and destroy these bugs. But be carful, doing so will fix the games AI, you will then have to destroy or avoid them in order to move on to the next stage

FPS Controlls

You will navigate the game using First Person Controller style coming accross all sorts of enemies along the way

W = Forward
A = Left
S = Backwards
D = Right
R = Reload
Space = Jump
Left Click = Shoot

Gruesome Boss Battle

If you manage to make your way through all the enemies you will come gace to face with the virus in a gruesome boss battle

In this last stage you will have to attack the boss while his shields are down, when they are up he will send the infected game AI to attack you
