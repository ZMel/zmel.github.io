Welcome To Parody

A game to mock all games

Introduction

Parody is a 3D first person shooter with minor puzzle elements.

Thematically, each level will attempt to emulate a different game genre, and will subtly parody certain clich√©s common to each of these genres. Supporting both single player and multiplayer modes, single-player consists of 3 distinct stages each themed after a different game genre, while multiplayer mode is deathmatch where 2 players face off against each other.

You will play as a game testing Robot trying to fix 3 games from a terrible virus

The virus has deployed bugs in each game, it's your job to find and destroy them. The bugs have corrupted each games AI, and there will be strange events and behaviour until they are destroyed.

The AI will fight back

Once you 'debug' the level, the game will come to life and attack you for being an outsider. Fight your way to the portal and get to the next stage!

FPS Controls

You will navigate the game using First Person Controller style coming across all sorts of enemies along the way

W = Forward
A = Left
S = Backwards
D = Right
R = Reload
Space = Jump
Left Click = Shoot
Exit = Brings up in game menu.

Gruesome Boss Battle

If you manage to make your way through all the enemies you will come face to face with the virus in an epic boss battle

In this last stage you will have to attack the boss while his shields are down, when they are up he will send the infected game AI to attack you.
